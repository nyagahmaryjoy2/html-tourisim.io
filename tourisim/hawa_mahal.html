Hawa Mahal

Hawa Mahal is a beautiful palace located in the heart of Jaipur. The palace was built in the 18th century by Maharaja Sawai Pratap Singh and is known for its unique architecture.

The palace is made of red sandstone and has a complex of windows and balconies that allow the royal ladies to observe the street life without being seen. The palace is also home to a beautiful garden and a museum.

Hawa Mahal is a popular tourist attraction in Jaipur and is known for its stunning architecture and beautiful gardens.
